Things to do in D.C.

What to do in D.C.? From sightseeing to ice skating, from apple picking to shopping, this page will give you the best things to do in D.C.!

On top of the Washington Monument

Located near the Ellipse, close to so many memorials and museums, lies the Washington Monument. Getting here is super easy. The closest metro stations are Smithsonian and Federal Triangle. After getting off, walked towards the monument.

Anyone can go to the monument, take pictures, and just sit outside, but only people with tickets can go inside the monument. There are two ways to get tickets. One is to get it online, another is to get to the monument early in the morning to get a ticket.

Once you got a ticket, and go in, you get into an elevator that will bring you up to 500 feet. There, you will be able to see a nice view of D.C. Once you get tired of looking, you go down one floor to the 490 feet level, and wait for the elevator to take you back down.

But, don't look at your phone when you go down, keep listening to the guide, cause you will have a chance to see the inside of the monument when you go down.
